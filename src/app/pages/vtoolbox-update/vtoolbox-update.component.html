Update.

Vtoolbox is now FounderTools. The original VToolbox was created over 2 years ago, and while it has served many users in its time, it is time for an upgrade.

This new iteration has improved tools, a better user interface and last but not least, a much better domain.

The intent of the original site was to create tools that would benifit Volusion store founders and administrators, and as such, I feel that the new domain better reflects those goals.
